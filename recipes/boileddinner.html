Ingredients:

  • 2 bone-in rib eye
  • 2-3 marrow bones
  • 1 side pork rib, cut into 3
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 carrot, peeled
  • 2 celery stalks, cut in half
  • 1 rutabaga, peeled and cut into wedges
  • 1 leek stalk, dark green removed
  • 4 Yukon gold potatoes, peeled
  • 4 turnips, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup pearl onions, peeled
  • 1 cup button mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp chopped thyme
  • salt

Directions:

  1. Place ribeye, bone marrow and pork ribs in a very large pot.
  2. Cover with cold water and heat over high.
  3. Allow scum to rise to surface of water.
  4. Remove by skimming the surface of the liquid with a ladle.
  5. Discard.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours
  7. Place chicken, vegetables and herbs in pot, cover and continue to cook over low heat until chicken is cooked through, about 1 more hour.
  8. Remove meat and vegetables from broth and set aside.
  9. Strain broth through a fine mesh sieve.
  10. Season with salt
  11. Slice meat.
  12. Place a few slices of the various meats and vegetables into shallow bowl, ladle broth over and serve.
