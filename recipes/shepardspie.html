SHEPHERD'S PIE RECIPE

  • 5 Yukon gold potatoes, peeled
  • 7 tbsp unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 1 cup pearl onion, peeled
  • 1 cup cremini mushrooms, diced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 cups leftover diced meat, like braised beef short rib
  • 2 cup veal demi glace
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 cup freezer peas

DIRECTIONS:

  1. potatoes until soft enough to effortlessly pierce with a paring knife, about 30 minutes.
  2. Drain
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high.
  4. Add in 3 tablespoons of butter, onion, carrot, celery pearl onion and mushrooms.
  5. ook until fragrant and tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add in tomato paste and beef stock. Continue to cook until liquid has reduced by half. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Stir in meat, demi-glace, and parsley.
  8. Preheat oven to 375F.
  9. Gently mash the potatoes with 3 tablespoons butter and season with salt.
  10. Continue to mash until the mixture is smooth and even.
  11. Place the meat mixture into a baking dish.
  12. Cover with mash potatoes.
  13. Using a spatula or fork, hatch a rough pattern into the surface.
  14. Bake in preheated oven until the surface of the pie is golden, about 1 hour.
  15. Remove from oven and let rest for a minimum of 15 min.
  16. Meanwhile, melt the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter in a small pot.
  17. Stir in peas until heated through.
  18. Serve with shepherds pie.
